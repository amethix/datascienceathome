In a nutshell

Data Science at Home is the blog for data scientists who want to build the right tools to do advanced analytics and machine learning. Data science practitioners and aficionados will get familiar with the beautiful art of data analysis, from algorithm design to their practical implementations.

While the major focus is on artificial intelligence and deep learning, Data Science at Home provides the best practices in collecting, cleaning and managing data, and other advanced machine learning techniques in domains like healthcare, finance, social media, and many more.
